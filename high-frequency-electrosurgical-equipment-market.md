<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The High Frequency Electrosurgical Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Circuit</li><li>Bipolar Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Frequency Electrosurgical Equipment includes two primary market types: monopolar circuit and bipolar circuit. </p><p>The monopolar circuit involves a single active electrode delivering current to the tissue, with a grounding pad to complete the circuit, making it suitable for cutting and coagulation. </p><p>In contrast, the bipolar circuit uses two electrodes on the surgical instrument itself, providing more localized energy transfer with minimized thermal spread, ideal for precise applications in delicate tissues. </p><p>Both types enhance surgical efficacy and patient safety.</p></p>
