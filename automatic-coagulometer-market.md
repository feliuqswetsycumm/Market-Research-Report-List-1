<p><p>The automatic coagulometer market encompasses various detection types for assessing blood coagulation. Photo-optical detection employs light transmission techniques to monitor clotting, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. Electrochemical detection utilizes electrical signals to gauge clot formation, appealing for its rapid response and compact design.
