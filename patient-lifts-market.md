<p><p>The patient lifts market consists of two main types: manual lifts and power lifts. Manual lifts require caregivers to use physical strength to assist patients, offering a cost-effective solution but demanding more effort and positioning skill. Conversely, power lifts are motorized, enabling easier transfers with minimal physical strain on caregivers, providing enhanced safety and efficiency. Both types are vital in healthcare settings to facilitate patient mobility, reduce the risk of injury, and ensure comfort during transfers.</p></p>
